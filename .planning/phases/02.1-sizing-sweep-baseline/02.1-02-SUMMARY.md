## Decisions Made

- Deduplication yields 322 unique combos (not ~308 as estimated in plan). The plan's estimate was approximate; 322 is the correct mathematical result of the dedup rules.
- `_get_filtered_bars()` re-applies the simulator's date/RTH filter to obtain filtered bar indices for the `winning_session_pct` computation. The simulator does not expose filtered bars as part of its SimulationResult contract.
- `max_dd_duration_bars` uses `duration_bars` as a proxy for bar timeline since the cycles DataFrame provides this column directly. Exact bar-level timestamps are not needed for computing relative duration of drawdown stretches.
