Write stages/05-assessment/assess.py — a simple script that:
- Reads the engine's result.json (or trade_log.csv if the engine writes it)
- Computes the Stage 05 reporting metrics:
  - Sharpe: mean(pnl_ticks) / std(pnl_ticks) * sqrt(n_trades) — trade-level, no annualization
  - Gross Sharpe (before cost) and net Sharpe (after cost_ticks per trade)
  - Confirms net Sharpe < 80% of gross Sharpe (realistic cost modeling)

  - Summary section: PF, n_trades, win_rate, Sharpe (gross and net)
  - Cost impact section: gross vs net PF and Sharpe, percentage reduction
  - Per-mode breakdown
  - Verdict: PASS/FAIL based on statistical_gates.md thresholds (or INSUFFICIENT_DATA if n_trades < 30)

The assess.py script is NOT the full Stage 05 assessment (that comes in later phases) — it is the minimum needed for ENGINE-07 end-to-end verification. Keep it under 80 lines.
