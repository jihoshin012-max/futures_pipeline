Step 4 of 6

Simulator & scoring

Two questions here: how are trades simulated, and how are signals scored before simulation runs.

What is a simulator?

The simulator takes each signal and replays it bar-by-bar — applying your stop, targets, and trail rules — to compute trade outcome. It's the engine that turns a signal list into P&L.

What is a scoring adapter?

Before simulation, each signal is assigned a score. Only signals above a threshold are simulated. The adapter is the module that computes that score from your features. If your strategy takes every signal without filtering, set score_threshold to 0.
